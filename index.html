Installing PharmaSmart Safely on Windows ⚠️

When you download software, Windows may display a SmartScreen warning. This feature is designed to protect users from unknown or unsafe programs. Because PharmaSmart is a new application and not yet widely distributed, Windows may not immediately recognize it, even though it is completely safe and malware-free.

How to Continue the Installation

  1. When the warning appears, click “More info”.
  2. You will see that the publisher is listed as PharmaSmart.
  3. Click “Run anyway” to start the installation.

PharmaSmart has been thoroughly checked before release. No harmful code is present, and it is safe to use in your pharmacy environment.

Your security is important to us. If you ever have doubts, you can contact our support team to verify the installer before proceeding.
