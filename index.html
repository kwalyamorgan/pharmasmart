Powerful Features to Streamline Your Pharmacy

Smart Inventory Management

Keep track of every item in real-time. Automatically generate once-daily alerts for low stock and expiring drugs, so you never miss a critical detail.

Efficient Point of Sale (POS)

Process sales quickly and accurately. The system automatically records patient and prescription details, ensuring a seamless and compliant checkout process.

Automated Compliance Reporting

Generate official, printable PDF reports for DDA, OTC, and Prescription-Only drugs with a single click, saving hours on manual paperwork.

Secure User & Data Management

Take full control with a dedicated admin panel. Manage users and passwords, and use the built-in backup and restore feature to protect your valuable data.

Vendor & Supplier Tracking

Easily add, edit, and manage all your vendor information in one place. Streamline your supply chain and maintain clear records of your business partners.

Comprehensive Deletion Logs

Maintain a complete and transparent history of all deleted records with a permanent log. This ensures accountability and provides a clear audit trail for any changes.
